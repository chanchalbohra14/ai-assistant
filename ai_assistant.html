📚 User Guide - How to Use Your AI Assistant

Available Functions:

  • Question Answering: Get factual information and explanations on various topics
  • Text Summarization: Summarize articles, documents, or long texts into key points
  • Creative Content: Generate stories, poems, essays, and creative writing
  • Study Advice: Receive personalized tips and strategies for effective learning

How to Use:

  • Select a function by clicking on one of the colored cards below
  • Choose from different prompt styles (varying in length, tone, and complexity)
  • Enter your specific query or text in the input area
  • Click "Generate Response" to see the AI's output
  • Provide feedback to help improve the assistant's performance
